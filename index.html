About Us

Welcome to The King's Court.

We are the official Chess Club of MBM University. Begin your jouney into the world of chess with “King's court” where we foster a love for the game and a sense of community among chess entusiasts of all levels, uniting players from beginners to advanced under one philosophy: strategize, compete, conquer.

Through regular events, puzzle challenges, and competitive tournaments, We aim to promote chess education, improve chess skills, build a community , organise tournaments and events while fostering leadership and teamwork.

Our Journey

Club Formation

Founded in 17th October 2023, “The King's Court” began its journey to build a community of chess lovers at MBM.

200+ Attendees Milestone

Within just 3 months, we crossed the 200-Attendees mark, showing the club's growing popularity.

Regular Offline Meetups

Started regular offline chess club meetups , 3 days-a-week.

Khelo MBM 2024 Organized

Hosted the chess event at the Khelo MBM,2024.

Regular Online Chess Tournaments

Started the online chess team at Lichess platform and started conducting regular online tournaments.

Khelo MBM 2025 Organized

Hosted with 30+ contenders in main event selected from 100+ participants after days of trials.
